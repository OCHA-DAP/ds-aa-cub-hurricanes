Dear national authorities,
Dear Resident Coordinator,
Dear OCHA ROLAC colleagues,

We want to inform you that the observation/early response trigger of the collective anticipatory action framework for hurricanes in Cuba has been reached, according to observational data issued during the last 48 hours by the NOAA National Hurricane Center and NASA IMERG precipitation data.

Additional updates will be provided as appropriate.

Thank you for your continued commitment to anticipatory action.

Sincerely,

OCHA Centre for Humanitarian Data

Contact Contact the OCHA Centre for Humanitarian Data through Raquel Peña, focal point for anticipatory action in Cuba at raquel.pena@un.org for any questions or comments.
